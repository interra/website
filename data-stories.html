Data Visualization and Story-Telling

Interra can take your data and transform it into compelling data visualizations and story telling applications.

We specialize in tellings stories of civic value or interest using open source tools. Contact us to hear more about how we can help unlock your open data.

React Dash

The React Dash library is our go-to tool for creating data visualizations and dashboards. It makes it easy to creat dashboards that allow users to drill down into data creating simple interfaces that explain complex relationships.
